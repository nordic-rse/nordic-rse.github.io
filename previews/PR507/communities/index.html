The community of research software engineers in the Nordics

We have a small but growing community distributed across the Nordic countries (and even beyond).

RSE groups in Nordic countries

To create a Nordic network of RSEs, we are interested in listing RSE groups, teams, and local communities. They may be based in (for example) universities, national labs, research institutes, academic departments, or university IT organisations. If you are organized in such a group or would like to be added here, please contact us.

  • Aalto Scientific Computing, Aalto University, Finland
  • IEDL - Industrial Ecology Digital Lab at NTNU Trondheim, Norway
  • NTNU Trondheim RSE Community, Norway
  • UIT - The Arctic University of Norway, Norway

Community map of research software engineers in the Nordics

Add yourself to the map

You can add yourself or your group to this map by sending a pull request changing the data file.

Who can be included? We don't strictly define it, but welcome anyone who feels a connection to the research software engineering spirit. You don't have to have an "RSE" job title, or even be recognized for your software expertise, but you should feel it relates to your current or future career somehow.

To add yourself, please read our map documentation.
